Featured Trips

Big Banjo Rapids

Dive into the heart-pounding excitement of Big Banjo Rapids, a wild and untamed river adventure that will leave you breathless. With its challenging Class IV and V rapids, this extraordinary river is a paradise for thrill-seekers. Navigate the surging waves and breathtaking drops while surrounded by the untouched beauty of towering pine forests and rugged cliffs.

Snaking River

Get ready for an epic journey down the expansive Snaking River. This grand river offers a thrilling, scenic experience that spans across a vast and diverse landscape. With a mix of Class II, III, and IV rapids, it's an excellent choice for intermediate and experienced rafters looking for excitement. You'll be awestruck by the dramatic canyons, towering cliffs, and pristine wilderness that surrounds you as you navigate the twists and turns of Snaking River. Our professional guides, well-versed in the nuances of this impressive waterway, ensure your safety and maximize your enjoyment throughout the adventure.

Little Lazy Suzie

Escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse yourself in the peaceful serenity of Little Lazy Suzie. This leisurely river journey is a perfect escape for those seeking a more relaxed and scenic rafting experience. Meandering through lush forests, this gentle river offers a soothing ride with Class I and II rapids, making it ideal for families and nature enthusiasts. Our friendly guides ensure your safety and enjoyment as you drift along the winding waters, taking in the beauty of the surrounding landscape.
