White Water Adventures

Our company purpose is to provide a safe and adventurous experience on the white water rivers of Utah for you and your family!

History

Our company was founded in 1899 by our founder John Doe. He and his sister, Jane, set out to go white water rafting and realized they need much more equipment than they originally thought. They started their rafting business with nothing but a set of old tire tubes and some limited experience on the water. After many trials on the river (and a couple of nasty lawsuits) the siblings felt ready to open the business to the public. At first they mostly catered to friends and family for steep discounts, but finally got a big break when the mayor of their small town decided to pay them a visit, after hearing about their business from their father. Since then it's all been easy waters!
